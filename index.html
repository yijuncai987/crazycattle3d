About CrazyCattle3D

CrazyCattle3D is a hilarious and chaotic indie physics-based battle royale game that has taken the internet by storm. Don't let the name fool you – in CrazyCattle3D, you play as a surprisingly agile sheep!

How to Play CrazyCattle3D? Your goal is simple: be the last sheep standing in the arena. Control your sheep using your mouse and WASD keys. Use momentum and strategic bumps to knock other sheep off the platform or into hazards. There are no weapons; success in CrazyCattle3D relies purely on your skill in maneuvering and timing your attacks. Ready to experience the wacky sheep mayhem?
